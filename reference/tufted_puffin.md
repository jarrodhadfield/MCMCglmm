# Diet composition of Tufted Puffin

Diet composition of Tufted Puffin (*Fratercula cirrhata*) chicks from
Middleton Island in the Gulf of Alaska (1978-2024).

## Usage

``` r
data(tufted_puffin)
```

## Format

a data frame with 565 rows and 7 columns. Each row corresponds to a
sample and `year` and `month` refer to the date of collection. The
following four columns are the number of Capelin (`Capelin`), Pacific
Herring (`Herring`), Walleye pollock (`Pollock`) and Pacific Sand lance
(`SandLance`) in each sample. An additinoal 40 prey taxa were also
recorded and their combined counts per sample are in the final column
(`Other`).

## Source

Hatch, S., Arimitsu, M., Piatt, J., Whelan, S., and Marsteller, C.E.,
2023, Seabird Diet Data Collected on Middleton Island, Gulf of Alaska
(ver 3.0, February 2025): U.S. Geological Survey data release,
<https://doi.org/10.5066/P93I0P67>.
