# Data on Acute Bee Paralysis Virus Infections in Bumblebees from Pascall et al. (2018).

Acute Bee Paralysis Virus infection status in Bumblebees from pooled
samples (Pascall et al. (2018)).

## Usage

``` r
data(ABPvirus)
```

## Format

A data frame with 99 rows and 3 columns. Pooled samples of bumblebees,
conisting of `number` individuals, were assayed for an Acute Bee
Paralysis virus infection. In samples where the virus was detected
`infected` is equal to one, or zero otherwise. The column `species`
gives the species' latin name (the genus is *Bombus*).

## References

Pascall, D.J. et al. 2018 BioRxiv 498717
