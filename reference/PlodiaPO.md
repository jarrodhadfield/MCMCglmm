# Phenoloxidase measures on caterpillars of the Indian meal moth.

Phenoloxidase measures on caterpillars of the Indian meal moth (*Plodia
interpunctella*).

## Usage

``` r
data(PlodiaPO)
```

## Format

a data frame with 511 rows and 3 columns, with variables indicating
full-sib family (`FSfamily`), phenoloxidase measures (`PO`), and plate
(`plate`). PO has undergone a Box-Cox power transformation of 0.141

## Source

Tidbury H & Boots M (2007) University of Sheffield
