# Infection Data for Sigma Virus in *Drosophila melanogaster*

Infection data for sigma virus isolates across lines of *Drosophila
melanogaster*

## Usage

``` r
data(sigma)
```

## Format

a data frame with 1092 rows and 4 columns. `infected` and `not_infected`
give the number of flies in a vial that are infected or not infected
with sigma virus. The viruses belong to one of five strains designated
by their country of origin: United States (AP30), Spain (GC20), Greece
(PF115), the United Kingdom (E27) and France (Hap23). The flies are
genetically identical except at the third chromosome for which they
belong to one of 67 lines `line`.
